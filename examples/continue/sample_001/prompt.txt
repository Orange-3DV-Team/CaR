An ancient East Asian street market with winding stone paths between layered wooden buildings, upturned eaves, and blue-gray tiled roofs. Warm lanterns hang along both sides of the street, swaying gently in the breeze and casting a soft glow. Blooming cherry trees scatter pale pink petals around red torii gates. Stalls are filled with goods, wooden barrels, and bamboo baskets. Tall pavilions and low houses form a rich skyline in the distance. The whole scene is bathed in morning light, quiet yet full of life, as if time has paused to tell an old and peaceful story.
